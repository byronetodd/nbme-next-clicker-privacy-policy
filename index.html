Privacy Policy for NBME Next Clicker

Effective Date: 07/04/2024

NBME Next Clicker is a Chrome extension designed to automate the process of navigating through questions in online tests hosted on starttest.com. This privacy policy outlines our practices regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of user data.

Data Collection and Use:

We do not collect, store, or share any personal data from users of the NBME Next Clicker extension. The extension operates entirely on the user's device and interacts with the content of the currently active tab on starttest.com to perform its functionality. No data is transmitted to external servers or third parties.

Permissions:

The NBME Next Clicker extension requires certain permissions to function correctly:

  • activeTab: This permission allows the extension to interact with the currently active tab when the user initiates its functionality. This is necessary to automate the navigation process.
  • scripting: This permission is essential for injecting and executing the content script within the active tab, enabling the automation of the "Next" button clicks.
  • Host Permissions (*://*.starttest.com/*): This permission allows the extension to access and manipulate the content of test pages hosted on starttest.com.

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page, and the effective date will be updated accordingly.
